The problem

  • Half of the plastic we use every day is thrown away within 20 minutes of use.

  • A quarter of a ton of plastic ends up in our ocean every second. That’s the equivalent of one full truck every minute; 1,440 trucks every day and 8,000,000 tons every year.

  • If we don’t change our ways, it is predicted that, in 2050, there will be more plastic than fish in the sea!
